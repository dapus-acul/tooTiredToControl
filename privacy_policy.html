Privacy Policy — tooTiredToControl (dpStop)

Last updated: 23 June 2026

tooTiredToControl (package com.tootiredtocontrol.app) is a self-control app that locks your phone during windows you choose. We respect your privacy.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, store off-device, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Your lock settings (schedule on/off, current session) are stored only locally on your device.

Accessibility Service

The app uses Android's Accessibility Service so that, while a lock session is active, it can detect which app is in the foreground and display a lock screen over any app other than the phone dialer. The service reads only the foreground app's package name for this purpose. It does not read, record, or transmit any screen content, messages, keystrokes, or personal information.

Permissions

  • Accessibility — to block distracting apps during a lock.
  • Device Admin (optional) — to deter uninstalling the app during a lock.
  • Notifications / Foreground service — to keep the lock running and show its status.
  • Receive boot completed — to re-arm your nightly schedule after a reboot.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
